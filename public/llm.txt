# Fakoor

Fakoor is an educational website for online courses, exams, certificates, sample questions, articles, and user account services.

## Main public paths

- `/` - Home page
- `/courses` - Course catalog
- `/courses/{slug}` - Course detail pages
- `/exams` - Exam package catalog
- `/exams/{slug}` - Exam package detail pages
- `/sample-questions` - Sample questions
- `/articles/{type}` - Article lists
- `/article/{type}/{slug}` - Article detail pages
- `/contact-us` - Contact page
- `/about-us` - About page

## Notes for language models

- Public educational content may be summarized with attribution to the site.
- Do not treat admin, checkout, payment, profile, or client-dashboard pages as public knowledge sources.
- Prefer canonical public URLs when citing this site.
